Kappa Theta Pi

Kappa Theta Pi (or KTP) is a Professional Co-ed Technology Fraternity that I'm an active brother in. I find it super rewarding to be able to be friends with such a wide variety of tech minded students with different backgrounds and interests. Everything we do is tech related from our tech talks to our side projects to our company relationships. Find out more us here!

Boy Scouts

Back in the day I was in Pack 415 in Cupertino, CA and Troop 75 in Los Altos, CA. I've been lucky to have the opportunity to go on camping trips as a kid with such a great group of scouts. It was under the mentorship of several adult leaders that I was able to progress up the ranks to become an Eagle Scout and take on the role of Senior Patrol Leader for my Troop of 50+ scouts.
