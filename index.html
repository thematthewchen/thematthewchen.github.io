MDP Project

Currently I'm part of a Multidisciplinary Design Program (MDP) Project Team called "Dynamic Kirigami Solar Cell System" that is revolutionizing the structure of solar cells. I am constructing the Android app that will be used as an interface to orient the solar cells upon and after installation.
