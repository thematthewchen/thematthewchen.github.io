Kirigami Solar Cell Android App

I constructed an Android App for an upcoming startup to use as an interface to control Kirigami Solar Cells through Google’s Firebase. The app has since been modified to communicate with an Arduino through Bluetooth uses Location and Position Sensing ability (angle of phone). To communicate with the Solar Cells I coded an algorithm that parses byte arrays received from solar panel and converts it into readable Strings.
